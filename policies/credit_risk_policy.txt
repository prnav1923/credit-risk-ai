CREDIT RISK ASSESSMENT POLICY DOCUMENT
Version 1.0 | Lending Operations Division

1. LOAN APPROVAL CRITERIA
- Minimum FICO score: 650
- Maximum DTI ratio: 43%
- Minimum annual income: $25,000
- Maximum loan amount: $40,000 for unsecured personal loans
- Employment length: Minimum 1 year preferred

2. RISK GRADE DEFINITIONS
- Grade A (Low Risk): FICO > 750, DTI < 15%, score < 0.2
- Grade B (Low-Medium Risk): FICO 700-750, DTI 15-20%, score 0.2-0.35
- Grade C (Medium Risk): FICO 670-700, DTI 20-30%, score 0.35-0.5
- Grade D (Medium-High Risk): FICO 640-670, DTI 30-35%, score 0.5-0.65
- Grade E-G (High Risk): FICO < 640, DTI > 35%, score > 0.65

3. AUTOMATIC DECLINE CRITERIA
- FICO score below 580
- DTI ratio above 50%
- Bankruptcies in last 2 years
- More than 3 delinquencies in last 2 years
- Revolving utilization above 90%

4. MANUAL REVIEW CRITERIA
- Risk score between 0.3 and 0.6
- Income not verified
- Loan purpose: small business or medical
- First time borrower with limited credit history
- Joint applications with co-borrower DTI concerns

5. INTEREST RATE POLICY
- Grade A: 5.99% - 8.99%
- Grade B: 9.99% - 12.99%
- Grade C: 13.99% - 16.99%
- Grade D: 17.99% - 20.99%
- Grade E: 21.99% - 25.99%
- Grade F-G: 26.99% - 30.99%

6. DEBT CONSOLIDATION LOANS
- Maximum consolidation amount: 85% of total existing debt
- Verification of existing debt required for amounts > $15,000
- DTI post-consolidation must be below 40%
- Preferred for borrowers with revolving utilization > 60%

7. HIGH RISK INDICATORS
- Revolving utilization above 75%
- Public records or bankruptcies present
- Multiple hard inquiries in last 6 months
- Delinquency history in last 2 years
- Loan to income ratio above 0.4

8. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
- All lending decisions must comply with Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
- Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) compliance required
- No discrimination based on race, gender, religion, national origin
- Adverse action notices required for all declined applications
- All decisions must be explainable and auditable

9. MODEL GOVERNANCE
- AI model predictions must be reviewed quarterly
- AUC-ROC below 0.70 triggers mandatory model review
- Feature importance must be logged for every prediction
- Model drift monitored via CloudWatch metrics
- Human override allowed for edge cases

10. COLLECTIONS POLICY
- 30 days past due: Automated reminder sent
- 60 days past due: Collections team notified
- 90 days past due: Loan charged off, sent to collections agency
- Recovery rate target: 35% of charged off amount
